# Code Of Conduct

This repository is a professional engineering project. Keep interactions
respectful, direct, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, and review comments.
- Focus on technical facts, reproduction steps, and concrete proposals.
- Assume good intent, but be willing to challenge ideas clearly and politely.
- Keep discussions on topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Repeated derailment, trolling, or bad-faith argument.
- Posting secrets, customer data, private robot data, or other sensitive
  material in public threads.

## Moderation

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these
expectations, and may restrict participation if needed to keep the project
usable.

If a conduct issue should not be handled in public, contact the maintainers
privately through the normal repository ownership channel.
